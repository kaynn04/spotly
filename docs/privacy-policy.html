Privacy Policy

Updated May 19, 2026
Offline-first inventory

Synop helps you organize spaces, containers, items, lending records, outside-session checklists, photos, warranty information, reminders, and related notes. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information Synop Stores

Synop stores the information you enter into the app, including item names, space and container names, lending details, warranty dates, notes, preferences, and photos you add. This information is stored locally on your device.

Photos, Camera, and Files

Synop may request access to your camera, photo library, and files so you can take photos, attach existing images, import app data, or export/share backups. Photos and selected files are used only for the features you choose inside the app.

Microphone and Voice Recognition

Synop may request microphone access for voice-command features. Voice recognition may use speech-recognition services available on your device or provided by your operating system. Synop does not create a user account or store microphone recordings on its own servers.

Notifications

Synop may request notification permission to send local reminders, such as lending or warranty reminders. Notification data is used to schedule reminders on your device.

Internet Access

Synop may include internet permission for platform compatibility and features that open external links, such as help or system-service links. Synop does not require an account and does not operate a backend service that collects your inventory data.

Data Sharing

Synop does not sell your personal information. The app does not share your locally stored inventory data with the developer unless you intentionally export or share that data outside the app.

Third-Party Services

Some device features, such as speech recognition, notifications, file sharing, and app-store services, may be provided by your device operating system or third-party platform services. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your Synop data remains on your device until you delete it, clear the app data, uninstall the app, or use an in-app reset/delete feature. Exported backups or shared files are managed by you wherever you choose to save them.

Children's Privacy

Synop is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer using the contact details on the Google Play Store listing.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the developer contact information listed on Synop's Google Play Store listing.
